# collabiora and Parkinson's Community Los Angeles Partner to Bring AI-Powered Health Information to Parkinson's Patients and Caregivers


March 31, 2026

---

LOS ANGELES, CA — collabiora, an AI-driven health information platform built for patients and caregivers, today announced a partnership with Parkinson's Community Los Angeles (PCLA) to launch its inaugural pilot program serving the Parkinson's disease community in Los Angeles.

The partnership will make collabiora's platform available to PCLA members — patients, care partners, and families — giving them a trusted tool to access plain-language explanations of medical research, discover relevant clinical trials including those in the UCLA pipeline not yet publicly listed, and connect with specialists matched to their specific needs.

collabiora was co-founded by Dr. Sanskriti Sasikumar, a movement disorders neurologist at UCLA, bringing firsthand clinical expertise to the platform's design and mission. The platform is built to close the gap between the pace of medical research and what patients and families can realistically find, read, and understand on their own.

> "People living with Parkinson's and their caregivers deserve access to the same quality of information that their doctors have — presented in a way they can actually use," said Dr. Sanskriti Sasikumar, co-founder and CEO of collabiora. "Our partnership with PCLA is a meaningful first step toward making that a reality."

PCLA members will be invited to join as founding beta participants, helping to shape the platform through direct feedback during this early phase. The pilot will serve as a model for collabiora's planned expansion to additional patient communities across Los Angeles, including partnerships with USC, Cedars-Sinai, and the Pacific Neuroscience Institute.

> "Partnering with collabiora aligns with PCLA's mission to empower people impacted by Parkinson's to live their absolute best lives. collabiora's platform is a tremendous asset as it offers our community access to resources in an easy-to-use and digestible way."

## About collabiora

collabiora is an AI-powered health literacy platform that helps patients and caregivers understand medical research, find clinical trials, and connect with the right specialists. Co-founded by Dr. Sanskriti Sasikumar, a movement disorders neurologist at UCLA, and Esther Feldman, a Policy Advisor and Researcher at the ARQ National Psychotrauma Centre, collabiora is built for people navigating complex diagnoses who need clear, trustworthy information to make informed decisions about their care. Powered by AI. Built for people.

## About Parkinson's Community Los Angeles

Parkinson's Community Los Angeles (PCLA) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit dedicated to helping people with Parkinson's disease and their families live their absolute best lives. They offer free support groups, educational online and in-person events, and resources focused on wellness, exercise, and navigating daily life with Parkinson's.
